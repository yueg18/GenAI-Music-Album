Featured Song: Teacher Appreciation Song

The first track on the album is a farewell piece dedicated to my son's preschool teacher, marking her last day. This song is a heartfelt thank you for the incredible dedication, care, and joy she has brought into our children's lives. In recognition of her significant impact, I have composed this song, with the hope that it brings as much joy to her as she has brought to us.
